Decentralized Insurance Applications of Blockchain and Implications for Users, Industry, and Regulators

Dr. Stephen Karpischek is the co-founder and CEO of Etherisc, a blockchain project building Decentralized Insurance Applications. Before Etherisc, he co-founded a blockchain education startup and worked on digital innovation for leading European companies in banking, telecommunications, and automotive. Dr. Karpischek is a regular speaker at blochain and insurance events and likes to educate decision makers on blockchain technology and its consequences for society. He recieved a PhD in information management from ETH Zurich.
